How to play:

Guess the color pattern within 6 tries.


  • → Each guess must consist of 4 colors (in any combination)
  • → The color tile will be highlighted depending on your guess
  • → Green dots signifies the color is in the right location
  • → Red dots signifies the color does not belong in that position
