How to play:

Guess the color pattern within 10 tries.

  • Each guess must consist of 4 colors (in any combination)
  • Green dots signifies a color is in a right position
  • Red dots signifies a color does not belong in a position

Congratulations

You've solved the daily colorcode! Come back tomorrow and play again!

Alternatively, you're welcome to play endless mode.

Sorry :(

You didn't complete the colorcode this time, try again tomorrow!
