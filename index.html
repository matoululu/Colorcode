How to play:

Guess to color pattern within 7 tries.


  • → Each guess must consist of 6 colors (in any combination)
  • → The color tile will be highlighted depending your guess
  • → Green highlight signifies the right color in the right order
  • → Yellow highlight only signifies that the color is present in the pattern.
